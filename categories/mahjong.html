About Mahjong Games

Mahjong is one of the world's most popular tile-based games, originating in China during the Qing dynasty. Traditional Mahjong is a game of skill, strategy, and calculation, typically played by four players. However, the digital versions we offer are primarily solitaire variants that can be enjoyed by a single player.

The most common online version is Mahjong solitaire (sometimes called Shanghai), where players need to match identical pairs of exposed tiles to remove them from the board. The challenge comes from the stacking pattern, as you can only select tiles that are not covered by others and have at least one side open.

We also offer exciting variants like Mahjong Connect, where you need to find matching tiles that can be connected by a line with no more than three turns. This adds a new strategic element to the classic matching gameplay.

Our Mahjong games feature beautiful tile designs, various layouts, and difficulty levels suitable for both beginners and experienced players. Whether you enjoy the classic experience or prefer a new twist on the traditional format, our collection has something for everyone.
