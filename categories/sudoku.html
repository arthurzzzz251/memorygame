About Sudoku Games

Sudoku is a logic-based, combinatorial number-placement puzzle. The objective is to fill a 9×9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3×3 subgrids that compose the grid contain all of the digits from 1 to 9.

Originally called Number Place, the game was created by Howard Garns, a retired architect and freelance puzzle constructor, and first published in 1979. The puzzle became widely popular in Japan in 1986 and gained international popularity in 2005.

Our collection of Sudoku games includes the classic version as well as exciting variants like Killer Sudoku, Sudoku X, and Samurai Sudoku. Each offers a unique twist on the classic rules, providing a fresh challenge for Sudoku enthusiasts.

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an expert seeking a tough mental workout, our range of difficulty levels ensures there's something for everyone.
